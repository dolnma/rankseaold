OperationL2P & Counter Strike: Global Offensive

OperationL2P is a strategy guide website dedicated to helping players get better at playing CS:GO. We provide tools such as our innovative grenade maps to make learning the game that much simpler.
CS:GO is a team-based first person shooter, based on the decade old franchise of Counter Strike. Players take roles on the opposing teams, terrorists and counter-terrorists. In order to win a match, players must either eliminate the entirety of the enemy team, or achieve the map's objectives. Players can use earned currency in past rounds in order to purchase weapons and equipment
